Randomness with Whole Numbers

I want a random number from 1 and 10, and I want it to be a whole number.

In column A, generate a random number from 0 to 9 with decimals 10*rand(), but wrap that in the ROUNDUP function to round it up!

Remember that the ROUNDUP(N,1) function takes two inputs.

Make sure you get only whole number outputs from 1 to 10.
